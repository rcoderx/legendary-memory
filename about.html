SnekCoin Saga Whitepaper

Introduction

Overview of SnekCoin Saga: A game inspired by the nostalgia of playing snake games on devices like the Nokia 3310. SnekCoin Saga is an innovative twist on this childhood classic, integrating blockchain technology to reward players. It aims to build a crypto community bonded by shared memories of sneaking to play these games. Purpose of the Whitepaper: To detail SnekCoin Saga's gameplay, its unique tokenomics, and outline the roadmap for future development.

Gameplay Mechanics

Inspired by Snake Xenzia: Retains the core mechanics of classic snake games, with increasing speed as the snake consumes more food and various modes of death.

Levels and Difficulty

General Gameplay: Players receive 10 lives daily, with additional lives obtainable by sharing the game on Twitter (10 lives per share). Classic Mode: The snake dies only by biting itself. No walls or obstacles. Classic Advanced (Coming Soon): Introduces walls. Death occurs by wall collision or self-biting. Scoring is increased by 50% compared to Classic. Pro (Coming Soon): Adds in-game obstacles to Classic Advanced. Scoring is 100% higher than Classic. Pro Advanced (Coming Soon): Features more obstacles and harmful food. Scoring is 200% higher than Classic.

Snake Avatars

Launch Avatars: Gorok: Leader of the Grok tribe. GTP: Leader of the Great Tribe of Python. Bald: Leader of the Gugul tribe. More avatars with unique abilities affecting points will be introduced, available for purchase or airdrop.

Blockchain Integration

Ethereum Wallet Integration: Uses Ethereum wallet connect for user identification; no write access required. Fresh wallets are acceptable. No gas fee for claiming rewards. Token Rewards and Transactions: Top 100-1000 players on the leaderboard will be rewarded monthly with $HISS tokens. The distribution is based on a player's total points relative to the sum of all players' points.

Tokenomics

ERC20 Token Rewards: Monthly distribution of 5% of (Game Pool Rewards 60%) SnekCoin ($HISS) to top players. Token Distribution and Utility: Total Supply: 10 million SnekCoin. Allocation: Rewards: 60% Sent to a rewards contract. Team: 10% Vested in a contract, with 10% released monthly. Marketing: 5% ( 80% vested, with 10% released monthly.) Liquidity: 5% (80% of presale funds allocated for liquidity) Presale: 10% Airdrop: 10%

Airdrop

30% of airdrop allocation is allocated to the referral program 30% Is allocated to early game players 40% is allocated to presale participants

Presale

10% Total supply is allocated for presale. 10 ETH will be the Softcap while 20ETH is the hardcap.

Presale Math

Note that presale allocation is double liquidity allocation. Presale buyers may have an advantage on launch day

Competitive Elements

Real-Time Leaderboard Daily Token Rewards for Top Players Score-Based Reward System

Roadmap

November-December 2023: Conception, design, and development phase. End of January 2023: Launch of remaining 3 modes, new avatars, SnekCoin Saga 2.0 with improved visuals, and at least one exchange listing. February 2024: Launch of SnekCoin NFTs, additional exchange listings, CMC and Coingecko listings. March 2024: Release of SnekCoin 3.0 with more gameplay modes, NFT integration. May 2024: Introduction of streaming, 3D pre-release, implementation of community suggestions. July 2024: Full whitepaper release, major exchange listings, enhanced web/mobile design, mobile app, finalized 3D/2D versions, community suggestions fully integrated.
